HeyGen Free Tier: What You Actually Get

HeyGen's free plan is a permanent tier, not a time-limited trial. No credit card is required to sign up. Here is exactly what the free plan includes as of April 2026:

  • 3 videos per month, each up to 3 minutes long
  • 720p resolution with a HeyGen watermark on every export
  • 500+ stock avatars and 1,000+ AI voices
  • Access to Avatar IV for a limited demo — enough to see the quality difference
  • No video translation, no voice cloning, no commercial rights

Is the free plan enough?

For evaluation, yes. You can test the interface, preview avatar quality, and render a few sample clips to see if HeyGen fits your workflow. For professional use, no. The watermark alone disqualifies free-tier videos from any external use case — client presentations, marketing videos, social media content, or training materials. The 720p resolution also looks noticeably soft on modern displays.

If you need a more generous free tier, Vidnoz offers 60 free credits per day with 1,900+ avatars. For non-avatar AI video, CapCut is completely free with no watermark.

Creator Plan ($29/month): Who It's For

The Creator plan is HeyGen's entry-level paid tier and the most popular plan for individual creators. Here is what $29/month gets you:

  • 200 credits per month (10 minutes of Avatar IV video, or more with standard avatars)
  • 1080p Full HD resolution, no watermark
  • 700+ stock avatars including Avatar IV options
  • Voice cloning from a short audio sample
  • Video translation with lip-sync dubbing (uses credits)
  • Commercial usage rights on all exports
  • Unlimited video creation (limited by credits, not a hard video cap)

Cost per minute on Creator

At $29/month with 200 credits, and Avatar IV consuming 20 credits per minute, you get approximately 10 minutes of premium avatar video per month at $2.90 per minute. Standard avatars consume fewer credits, stretching your budget further. If you produce 2-3 short videos per week (under 2 minutes each), the Creator plan covers your needs comfortably.

Annual billing drops the price to $24/month ($288/year), reducing your effective cost per Avatar IV minute to about $2.40. That is competitive given that hiring a spokesperson for a single 2-minute video easily costs $200-500+.

Best for: Solo creators, freelance marketers, small business owners producing 2-4 short avatar videos per month. For a full feature walkthrough, see our HeyGen review.

Pro Plan ($99/month): Power Users

The Pro plan is designed for individual power users who need heavy credit usage — high-volume avatar video production, frequent video translation, or Interactive Avatar streaming. At $99/month ($79/month annual), Pro includes:

Best for: Agencies, content studios, and individual producers creating 10+ videos per month or translating existing video libraries into multiple languages.

Business Plan ($149/month): Teams

The Business plan is built for teams and organizations. At $149/month plus $20 per additional seat, it includes:

  • 1,000 credits per month in a shared team pool
  • 4K resolution output for broadcast and large displays

One thing to note: the Business plan has fewer credits (1,000) than the Pro plan (2,000) despite costing more. You are paying for team features, 4K output, and brand kit — not raw credit volume. If you are a solo user who needs maximum credits, Pro is the better choice. If you need multiple seats and brand controls, Business justifies the premium.

Best for: Marketing teams of 3-10 people, agencies managing multiple client accounts, and corporate departments producing branded video content at scale.

Enterprise makes sense when you need compliance features (SSO, audit logs), guaranteed uptime SLAs, or when your monthly credit consumption exceeds what the Business plan provides cost-effectively. How HeyGen's Credit System Works

This is where most HeyGen pricing confusion happens. HeyGen does not charge per video or per minute directly. Instead, every action that generates media consumes credits from your monthly pool.

Action Credit Cost Notes
Avatar IV video ~20 credits/min Premium lip-sync, micro-expressions
Standard avatar video ~1 credit/min Good quality, less realistic than Avatar IV
Video translation Varies by length Includes lip-sync dubbing, higher cost for long videos
Interactive Avatar (streaming) Credits per session Real-time, higher consumption rate
Video Agent 2.0 ~2 credits/min (API) Automated script-to-video pipeline
Talking Photo ~1 credit/min Animate a static photo to speak

Key rules to know:

  • Credits do not roll over — use them or lose them each billing cycle
  • You can buy additional credits if you run out mid-month (pricing varies)
  • Avatar IV and video translation are the biggest credit consumers
  • Standard avatars use significantly fewer credits — if Avatar IV realism is not essential for every video, mixing avatar types stretches your budget

Hidden Costs and What's NOT Included

HeyGen's pricing page is clear about plan rates but less transparent about these additional costs:

1. Credit overages

If you exhaust your monthly credits, you can purchase additional credits at a per-credit rate that is typically higher than your plan's effective rate. This adds up fast if you underestimate your video production volume.

2. API pricing is separate

The HeyGen API follows a pay-as-you-go credit model, billed independently from your web plan credits. API pricing starts at $5 for developers. If you plan to integrate HeyGen into your product, budget for API costs separately.

3. Custom avatar creation

While Business and Enterprise plans include custom avatar creation, the process requires you to record specific footage. If you hire a videographer or studio to capture that footage, that is an additional expense HeyGen does not cover.

4. No built-in video editor

HeyGen generates videos but lacks a timeline editor, transitions, or motion graphics. Most users pair HeyGen with Descript ($24/month) or another editor. Factor that into your total stack cost. See our solo creator stack guide for the full workflow.

5. Annual plan lock-in

Annual billing saves 17-20%, but you pay upfront and cancellation after the first 14 days is non-refundable. Multiple Trustpilot reviewers report difficulty obtaining refunds. Start monthly to validate your usage, then switch to annual once you confirm HeyGen fits your workflow.

6. Video translation eats credits fast

Translating a 5-minute video into one additional language can consume a significant chunk of your monthly credits. If you plan to localize content into 5+ languages, budget for the Pro plan or additional credit purchases.
