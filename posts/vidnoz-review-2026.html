What Is Vidnoz?

Vidnoz is a cloud-based AI video creation platform built around AI avatars, text-to-video generation, and a broad set of AI-powered video tools. The platform offers over 1,900 AI avatars, 2,000+ AI voices, and 2,800+ templates across 140+ languages. It positions itself as the budget-friendly alternative to premium avatar platforms like Synthesia and HeyGen.

What makes Vidnoz unusual in this market is its free tier. While most AI avatar platforms either have no free plan or offer a one-time trial, Vidnoz gives users 60 credits per day, enough for roughly 3 minutes of video. Those credits reset daily, which means you can produce avatar videos indefinitely without paying. The trade-off is 720p resolution and a watermark, but for testing and light production use, it is a genuinely usable free offering.

Beyond standard avatar videos, Vidnoz has expanded into a suite of AI tools that most competitors do not offer: face swap, talking photos, AI dance generator, video translation with lip-sync dubbing, and voice cloning. Some of these feel experimental, but they add real flexibility for creators who want to do more than just talking-head avatar content. The platform is ISO/IEC 27001:2022 certified, which is a meaningful trust signal for businesses handling sensitive content.

Key Features: What Vidnoz Offers

We tested Vidnoz across multiple workflows including avatar video creation, voice cloning, video translation, and several of its unique AI tools. Here is what the platform delivers.

1,900+ AI Avatars

The largest avatar library we have seen on any platform. Avatars span different genders, ages, ethnicities, and professional styles. You can use pre-built avatars on the free plan (1,800+) and access the full library on paid plans. Custom avatar creation is available on higher tiers. The variety is impressive, though individual avatar quality varies.

Text-to-Video

Paste a script and Vidnoz generates a complete video with your chosen avatar presenting the content. The workflow is straightforward: select an avatar, pick a voice, paste your script, choose a template, and generate. Output quality is good for social media and internal communications. The AI handles pacing and scene transitions automatically.

Voice Cloning

Available on the Business plan and above. You need only 10-20 seconds of audio to create a voice clone, and the cloned voice can speak in 140+ languages. This is one of the lowest audio requirements we have seen for voice cloning. The output is recognizable but lacks the emotional range of your natural voice, similar to most AI cloning tools.

Video Translation

Upload a video and Vidnoz translates it into another language with lip-sync dubbing. The platform supports 140+ languages for translation. This is a Business plan feature and is particularly useful for global businesses that need to localize training or marketing videos without re-recording. Lip-sync accuracy is reasonable for most language pairs.

Face Swap

A unique feature that most competitors do not offer. Upload a photo and swap faces in videos or images. The results vary in quality depending on lighting, angles, and source material, but the technology works well enough for creative and entertainment content. This is one of Vidnoz's genuine differentiators.

Talking Photos

Upload a static photo and animate it to speak with any of the 2,000+ AI voices. Useful for creating content from historical figures, product mascots, or personal photos. The mouth animation is detectable as AI-generated but effective enough for social media content and educational materials.

2,800+ Templates

Pre-designed video templates organized by use case: marketing, education, social media, e-commerce, corporate training, and more. Templates give you a polished starting point and are especially useful if you are not a designer. Quality is mixed, with some templates looking professional and others feeling dated.

AI Dance Generator

Upload a photo and the AI animates it into a dancing video. This is clearly an entertainment-focused feature, but it has genuine utility for social media creators looking for eye-catching content. The animations are fun and shareable, though obviously AI-generated. Not available on every competitor.

Team Collaboration and Enterprise Features

Vidnoz supports team collaboration with shared workspaces accommodating up to 1,000 members on enterprise plans. The platform also offers SCORM export for eLearning content, making it relevant for L&D teams building training materials. Commercial usage rights are included on all paid plans, and the ISO/IEC 27001:2022 certification provides an additional layer of trust for businesses handling sensitive or regulated content.

Free Tier: What You Actually Get

Vidnoz's free tier is the most generous we have seen among AI avatar platforms. Here is exactly what is included and what the limitations are.

  • 60 credits per day (roughly 3 minutes of video), resetting every 24 hours
  • 1,800+ AI avatars (out of the full 1,900+ library)
  • ~890 AI voices (out of 2,000+ total)
  • 720p resolution (paid plans unlock 1080p)
  • Watermark on all exported videos
  • No credit card required to sign up or use
  • Credits do not roll over — use them or lose them each day

The daily credit reset is unique in this market. Most platforms give you a monthly allowance or a one-time trial. Vidnoz's approach means you can produce a small amount of content every day indefinitely. For a faceless YouTube channel or social media account that publishes one short video per day, the free plan is genuinely workable, assuming you can live with the watermark and 720p resolution.

The practical limitation is that 60 credits gets you roughly one 3-minute video. If you need longer content or batch production, you will hit the daily limit quickly. And since credits do not roll over, you cannot save up for a larger project. The free plan is designed for consistent daily use, not burst production.

A Note on the Trustpilot Rating

We want to address the Trustpilot score directly because it is an important data point. Vidnoz has a 2.3 out of 5 rating on Trustpilot, with 39% of reviewers giving 5 stars and 42% giving 1 star. This extreme polarization is unusual and worth investigating. The positive reviews consistently praise the free tier, avatar variety, and ease of use. The negative reviews frequently cite billing issues, difficulty canceling subscriptions, and customer support responsiveness. On G2, Vidnoz scores approximately 4.5 out of 5, which paints a very different picture.

Our interpretation: the product itself delivers solid value for the price, but the customer support and billing experience has clear room for improvement. If you try Vidnoz, start with the free tier and evaluate thoroughly before committing to a paid plan. If you do subscribe, document your subscription details and use a payment method with chargeback protection.
