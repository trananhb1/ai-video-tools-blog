What Is InVideo?

InVideo is an AI-powered online video creation platform that has been around since 2020 and has steadily built one of the largest user bases in the AI video space. It positions itself as the tool for people who need professional-looking videos without professional-level editing skills. Think of it as the Canva of video, but more purpose-built and more capable when it comes to actual video production.

The platform offers two main workflows. The first is its AI video generator, where you type a prompt or paste a script and the AI produces a complete video with stock footage, music, captions, and transitions. The second is its template-based editor, where you pick from over 5,000 pre-designed templates and customize them with your own text, media, and branding. Both approaches are designed to get you to a finished video as quickly as possible.

InVideo has made a big push into AI features throughout 2025 and 2026, and the current version is significantly more capable than what existed even a year ago. The AI script generator, automatic scene matching, and text-to-video pipeline have all improved noticeably. But the core philosophy remains the same: make video creation accessible to everyone, regardless of technical skill.

Who Is InVideo For?

InVideo casts a wider net than most AI video tools. While platforms like Zebracat focus specifically on ad creatives and HeyGen specializes in avatar-based videos, InVideo tries to serve the general "I need a video and I do not know how to make one" market. Its sweet spot includes:

  • Small business owners who need marketing videos, product promos, and social content but cannot justify hiring a video editor
  • Social media managers creating content for Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Facebook, and LinkedIn on a daily or weekly basis
  • Marketing teams at small to mid-sized companies that need to produce video at volume without a dedicated creative department
  • Content creators and solopreneurs who want polished-looking videos for their channels, courses, or email campaigns
  • Real estate agents, coaches, and consultants who need quick promo videos and client-facing content

If you fall into any of these categories and the thought of opening Premiere Pro or DaVinci Resolve makes you break out in a cold sweat, InVideo was built for you.

Key Features: What InVideo Does Well

We spent several weeks testing InVideo across different use cases, from social media clips to marketing videos to educational content. Here is what stands out.

AI Video Generator (Text-to-Video)

Type a prompt like "create a 60-second Instagram Reel about the benefits of meditation" and InVideo generates a complete video with relevant stock footage, background music, text overlays, and transitions. You can also paste a full script and the AI breaks it into scenes automatically. The results are surprisingly usable out of the box.

5,000+ Templates

This is InVideo's biggest differentiator by volume. The template library covers virtually every use case: social media posts, ads, intros, outros, testimonials, product demos, real estate listings, event invitations, and more. Each template is fully customizable, and most are available in multiple aspect ratios.

iStock Media Library

Paid plans include access to a massive library of iStock photos and video clips directly within the editor. This is a genuine value-add because iStock content is typically premium-priced. Having it bundled into your subscription saves money and eliminates the friction of sourcing footage separately.

Brand Presets

Save your brand colors, fonts, logos, and intro/outro sequences as presets. Apply them across any template with one click. For businesses producing regular content, this ensures visual consistency without manually adjusting every new video.

AI Script Generator

Not sure what to say in your video? InVideo's built-in AI script writer generates scripts based on your topic, audience, and desired tone. It is not going to replace a professional copywriter, but it is genuinely helpful for overcoming blank-page syndrome and getting a solid first draft to work with.

Auto-Captions & Voiceover

Automatic caption generation with customizable styles, plus a built-in AI voiceover feature with multiple voices and languages. Given that the majority of social video is consumed on mute, auto-captions are essential, and InVideo handles them cleanly. The voiceover quality is adequate for most marketing content.

Multi-Platform Export

Export in 16:9 (YouTube, landscape), 9:16 (TikTok, Reels, Shorts), and 1:1 (social feed) from the same project. The platform automatically adjusts layouts and text positioning for each aspect ratio, saving you from rebuilding the same video three times for different platforms.

Collaboration Features

Share projects with team members, leave comments, and collaborate in real time. This is particularly useful for agencies or marketing teams where multiple people need to review and approve videos before publishing. Not groundbreaking, but a welcome addition over solo-only tools.

Who Should Use InVideo

Based on our testing, these are the use cases where InVideo delivers the most value.

1. Small Business Owners Creating Their Own Marketing Videos

If you run a local business, online store, or service-based company and need promotional videos but have zero editing experience, InVideo is tailor-made for you. Pick a template that matches your industry, swap in your text and logo, choose from the stock footage library, and export. You can have a professional-looking promo video in 15 minutes without learning any software.

2. Social Media Managers Producing Content at Volume

When you need to post video content across multiple platforms multiple times per week, speed matters more than perfection. InVideo's template system and multi-platform export let you maintain a consistent posting schedule without spending your entire day in an editor. The AI text-to-video feature is particularly useful for generating quick content around trending topics.

3. Marketing Teams That Need Volume Over Bespoke Quality

If your strategy depends on producing 20 video variations to test rather than one perfect video, InVideo's speed and template depth make it ideal. You can produce a batch of ad creatives, social clips, or email video content in a single afternoon. The brand presets ensure everything stays on-brand even when you are moving fast.

4. Course Creators and Coaches

For promotional content, lesson intros, testimonial compilations, and social proof videos, InVideo's templates cover these formats well. It is not a replacement for screen recording or lecture capture, but for all the marketing and supplementary content around your course, it does the job efficiently.

Who Should NOT Use InVideo

InVideo is not the right tool for everyone. Skip it if:

  • You are a professional video editor. InVideo will feel limiting. It does not offer timeline-level control, advanced color grading, keyframe animations, or the precision that tools like Premiere Pro or DaVinci Resolve provide. This is a template-first platform, not an editing suite.
  • You need AI avatar presenters. InVideo does not offer AI-generated talking-head avatars. If you need a virtual presenter for training videos, explainers, or personalized sales outreach, use HeyGen or Synthesia instead.
  • You need cinematic production quality. InVideo produces good-looking marketing content, not cinematic masterpieces. If your brand demands a premium, hand-crafted feel, you need a human editor or a professional production workflow.
  • You want highly original, non-template-based content. While the AI generator helps, InVideo's strength is its templates, and templates by nature can feel familiar. If standing out visually is critical to your brand, you may find the output too similar to what everyone else is producing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is InVideo good for beginners with no video editing experience?

Yes. InVideo is one of the most beginner-friendly AI video makers available. Its text-to-video feature lets you type a prompt or paste a script and the AI generates a complete video with stock footage, music, captions, and transitions. The template library also makes it easy to drag, drop, and customize without learning complex editing software.

Does InVideo offer a free plan?

Yes. InVideo offers a free plan that lets you create and export videos with an InVideo watermark. It includes access to the AI video generator and a limited selection of templates and stock media. You will need a paid plan starting at $25 per month to remove the watermark and unlock the full iStock media library.

How does InVideo compare to Canva for video creation?

InVideo is purpose-built for video creation, while Canva is a general design tool that includes video features. InVideo offers more video-specific templates, a deeper stock footage library powered by iStock, dedicated AI video generation from text prompts, and more granular control over video elements like transitions and timing. If video is your primary need, InVideo is the stronger choice.

Can I use InVideo for business and commercial purposes?

Yes. All paid InVideo plans include commercial usage rights. The iStock media library included with paid plans is fully licensed for commercial use, including social media ads, YouTube content, client work, and marketing materials. The free plan exports include a watermark, which makes them unsuitable for most commercial purposes.

What aspect ratios does InVideo support?

InVideo supports all major aspect ratios including 16:9 for YouTube and landscape videos, 9:16 for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts, and 1:1 for social media feed posts. You can choose your aspect ratio at the start of a project or switch between them during editing.
