What Is DeepBrain AI Studios?

DeepBrain AI Studios is a cloud-based AI video creation platform developed by DeepBrain AI, a company founded in 2016 and headquartered in Seoul, South Korea with offices in San Francisco. The platform enables users to produce professional-quality videos featuring AI avatars by simply typing or pasting text — no camera, studio, or production crew required.

What makes DeepBrain AI Studios distinctive in the crowded AI avatar space is its deeply integrated ChatGPT-powered workflow. While other platforms require you to write a script externally and then paste it in, DeepBrain lets you generate scripts directly inside the editor from a text prompt, a document upload, a URL, or even a raw idea. The platform handles script structuring, avatar selection, scene composition, and rendering in a single pipeline. This idea-to-video workflow is genuinely faster than any competitor we have tested.

The platform offers over 100 stock AI avatars with support for 150+ languages and 1,000+ AI voices. Users can also create custom full-body avatars from video recordings or photos — a feature where DeepBrain is one of the few platforms to support head-to-toe avatar generation with gesture control. The company counts Samsung, Hyundai, and other major enterprises among its clients, and has received backing from Microsoft for Startups through Azure. Key Features: What DeepBrain AI Studios Offers

We tested DeepBrain AI Studios across avatar creation, script generation, multilingual dubbing, and custom avatar workflows. Here is what the platform delivers in 2026.

ChatGPT Script Assistant

DeepBrain's standout differentiator. The integrated ChatGPT assistant generates complete video scripts from a text prompt, uploaded document, website URL, or article link. You describe what you want and the AI writes, structures, and formats the script ready for avatar delivery. This eliminates the biggest bottleneck in AI video production: writing the script itself.

AI Avatars & Custom Avatars

Choose from 100+ stock avatars spanning diverse genders, ethnicities, and styles, or create custom avatars from your own video recordings or photos. DeepBrain is one of the few platforms offering full-body, head-to-toe custom avatars with adjustable gestures, outfits, and expressions. Custom avatars speak in 150+ languages with natural lip-sync.

AI Dubbing with Lip-Sync

Translate and dub existing videos into multiple languages with automatic lip-sync technology. The Personal plan includes 120 minutes of dubbing per month and the Team plan includes 240 minutes. Audio-only dubbing is available on the free tier. Quality is strong for business use cases like training and marketing content.

Document & URL to Video

Convert PDFs, PowerPoint presentations, blog posts, or any URL into a complete AI avatar video. The platform analyzes the content, extracts key points, generates a script, and produces a video with appropriate avatars and scenes. This is particularly useful for repurposing existing content into video format at scale.

Interactive AI Avatars

Build real-time conversational avatar experiences powered by custom LLMs. Use cases include customer support chatbots with a human face, interactive training modules, and kiosk-based concierge services. The interactive avatar can be embedded on websites and responds to user queries in real time.

Multi-Language Support

Over 150 languages supported with 1,000+ AI voices in different accents and styles. Switch the spoken language of any video with a few clicks, eliminating traditional localization costs. Combined with lip-sync dubbing, this makes DeepBrain a strong choice for global teams producing multilingual content.

Generative AI Credits

DeepBrain includes generative credits for AI-powered features like image generation, background creation, and enhanced scene composition. The Personal plan provides 60 credits per month, while the Team plan provides 150 per seat. These credits power the more advanced AI generation features beyond basic avatar video.

Enterprise Features

Enterprise plans include SAML SSO, SCORM export for eLearning platforms, interactive quizzes embedded in videos, bulk video generation via API, and dedicated technical support. The SCORM export is notable — this puts DeepBrain alongside Synthesia as one of the few avatar platforms supporting native LMS integration.

The Workflow Advantage

Where DeepBrain AI Studios genuinely excels is workflow efficiency. The platform's ChatGPT integration means you can go from a rough idea to a finished video without leaving the editor. In our testing, we produced a 3-minute product explainer video in under 10 minutes, including script generation, avatar selection, and rendering. Competing platforms like HeyGen and Synthesia require you to arrive with a finished script, which adds a separate production step. For teams producing high volumes of content, this integrated workflow translates to meaningful time savings.

The No-Preview Problem

The inability to preview videos before rendering is DeepBrain's most significant workflow limitation. With competing platforms, you can typically preview how the avatar will look and sound before committing to a full render. With DeepBrain, every revision requires a complete re-render, which adds time to the production cycle. For short videos this is a minor inconvenience, but for longer content where you are iterating on timing and delivery, it becomes a genuine productivity drain. This is an area where we hope to see improvement in future updates.

If avatar realism is your top priority, choose HeyGen. If you need the deepest enterprise L&D toolset, choose Synthesia. If budget is the primary constraint, choose Vidnoz.
