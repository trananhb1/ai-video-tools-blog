What is HappyHorse 1.0?

HappyHorse 1.0 is a text-to-video and image-to-video model from Alibaba. It was first spotted as an anonymous entry on the Artificial Analysis Video Arena in early April 2026 under the codename "happyhorse" — the same stealth-launch pattern Anthropic used with Claude on the LMSYS Chatbot Arena. By April 10, the model had climbed to #1 on both the text-to-video and image-to-video boards, and Alibaba confirmed authorship shortly after. The team is most likely Tongyi Lab, the group that shipped the open-source Wan 2.1 and Wan 2.2 video models in 2025.

The model is API-only at launch — there is no consumer chat interface like Sora had. It will go live as a paid endpoint on fal.ai on April 30, 2026. fal.ai's standard premium video pricing runs $0.30–$1.20 per generated second; Alibaba has not yet confirmed final HappyHorse rates. There is no announced free tier, no waitlist, and no invite system — the model launches as a paid endpoint and you pay per generation.
