How to Stack Savings

Getting the best price on AI video tools is not just about finding a coupon code. Here are five strategies that can save you 30% to 60% compared to paying full monthly rates.

1. Always Choose Annual Billing

Every AI video tool charges less per month on annual plans. The savings typically range from 20% to 40%. A tool that costs $30 per month drops to $18–$24 per month with annual billing. Only commit to annual once you have tested the tool and know it fits your workflow.

2. Start on the Free Tier, Then Upgrade Strategically

Never pay for a tool you have not tested. Use the free tier or trial first. Once you are sure you need paid features, watch for seasonal promotions — Black Friday, New Year, and back-to-school periods often bring the deepest discounts of the year.

If you are a student, teacher, or part of a nonprofit organization, many tools offer 25% to 50% off standard pricing. Synthesia, HeyGen, and Descript all have education programs. Contact their sales teams with proof of eligibility.
