## GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) Requirements

Every new article MUST include:

### 1. Quick Answer Box (top of article, after H1)
- 3-5 sentence summary with specific numbers (price, score, date verified)
- Formatted for AI extraction: "As of [Month Year], [Tool] costs $X/mo..."
- Must answer the primary query in the first 2 sentences

### 2. Question-Shaped H2s
- Use "Is [Tool] worth it in 2026?" not "Value Assessment"
- Use "How much does [Tool] cost?" not "Pricing Overview"
- Use "Which is better, [A] or [B]?" not "Comparison Results"

### 3. Structured Data (JSON-LD)
- SoftwareApplication schema for every review
- FAQ schema for every FAQ section
- Review schema with rating, price, author
- BreadcrumbList for navigation

### 4. Specific, Citable Data Points
- Exact pricing with billing period specified
- "Verified [Date]" timestamps
- Feature counts (e.g., "120+ templates" not "many templates")
- Benchmark numbers where possible

### 5. llms.txt Updates
- Add every new article to llms.txt within 24 hours of publishing
- Include Quick Answer in the llms.txt entry
- Categorize under appropriate section
