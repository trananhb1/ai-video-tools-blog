Frequently Asked Questions

Can I request a review of a specific AI video tool?

Absolutely. Use the form above and select "Tool Review Request" as the subject. We prioritize tools that our readers ask about most. We currently cover 16 individual tool reviews and 12 comparison articles, with new content published weekly.

Do you offer sponsored reviews?

No. All of our reviews are independently conducted and funded. We never accept payment in exchange for a positive review. Our editorial process is fully transparent — read about how we review tools and our Affiliate Disclosure for full details.

I found an error in one of your reviews. How can I report it?

We appreciate corrections. Select "Report an Error or Correction" in the form above, and include the article URL and the specific detail that needs updating. We verify all reported errors against the current version of the tool and update our reviews promptly.

How often do you update your reviews?

We update reviews whenever a platform releases a significant update — new AI models, pricing changes, major feature additions, or UI overhauls. Every article shows its last-updated date so you know how current the information is. As of April 2026, we maintain active paid accounts on the major platforms we cover.

How do you make money?

AI Video Picks earns revenue through affiliate partnerships with some of the tools we review. When you sign up for a tool through one of our links, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. This revenue funds our tool subscriptions and allows us to keep producing free, in-depth content. Affiliate relationships never influence our ratings or recommendations. See our full disclosure.
