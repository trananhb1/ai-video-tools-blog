Our Editorial Process

Every review and comparison on AI Video Picks follows a structured, repeatable methodology. Here is exactly how we produce content:

1. Research & Account Setup

Before writing a single word, we sign up for the tool with a paid plan. We explore every menu, setting, and feature the platform offers. We also check the tool's documentation, changelog, and community forums to understand recent updates.

2. Standardised Testing

We run each tool through the same set of tests so our comparisons are fair:

  • Avatar/presenter quality: We create a 60-second presentation video using the same script across platforms, comparing lip sync, gesture naturalness, and visual fidelity.
  • Voiceover accuracy: We test text-to-speech with identical paragraphs, evaluating pronunciation, pacing, and emotional tone.
  • Editing workflow: We time how long it takes to go from script to finished video, noting friction points and workarounds.
  • Export quality: We examine output resolution, frame rate, compression artefacts, and file sizes.
  • Pricing value: We calculate cost per minute of output video at each pricing tier.

3. Writing & Fact-Checking

Every article includes current pricing (verified against the tool's website within 7 days of publication), feature availability by plan tier, and clearly dated information so you know how fresh the content is. We include "As of [Month Year]" markers throughout.

4. Regular Updates

AI video tools change fast. We revisit published reviews when platforms release significant updates — new AI models, pricing changes, feature additions, or major UI overhauls. Every article shows its last-updated date.

5. Rating Criteria

We evaluate every tool across five consistent dimensions:
