Who We Are

AI Video Picks is an independent review site founded and run by Tom, a tech enthusiast and content creator who has spent years working at the intersection of video production and artificial intelligence.

After spending countless hours testing AI video platforms for my own projects, I realized there was a gap in the market: most "reviews" were thinly disguised ads, and it was nearly impossible to get an honest, side-by-side comparison of these tools. So I built AI Video Picks to be the resource I wish I had when I started.

How We Review

Every review on AI Video Picks follows a consistent, thorough process:

  • Sign up and pay: We create our own account on every platform we review. We do not rely on free trials alone — we test paid plans to understand the full feature set.
  • Create real content: We produce actual videos using each tool, testing avatar quality, voiceover accuracy, template variety, editing features, and export options.
  • Document everything: We capture real screenshots and record our workflow so you can see exactly what the experience looks like.
  • Rate consistently: We evaluate every tool across the same criteria — ease of use, output quality, feature depth, pricing value, and customer support.
  • Update regularly: AI video tools evolve fast. We revisit and update our reviews as platforms release significant updates.
