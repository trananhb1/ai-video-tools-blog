## 1. HARO / Journalist Request Platforms

HARO (originally by Peter Shankman) was rebranded to Connectively by Cision, which then **shut down permanently on December 9, 2024**. The landscape has fragmented into multiple platforms. No single platform has enough volume anymore -- you need to monitor 4-5 platforms daily.

### Active Platforms (Priority Order)

#### A. Source of Sources (SOS) -- TOP PRIORITY
- **URL:** https://www.sourceofsources.com/
- **Cost:** Completely free (Peter Shankman, original HARO founder, created this)
- **How it works:** Sign up with name + email. Receive 1-3 daily email digests of journalist queries. Reply directly to the reporter if you have relevant expertise.
- **Categories:** Technology, Business & Finance, Lifestyle & Entertainment, Healthcare, Travel, General
- **Why #1:** Highest dofollow link rate of any platform at **36.26%**. Honor system -- off-topic pitching = immediate ban. ~40,000 members. High query quality, low spam.
- **Sign up:** Just name + email at sourceofsources.com
- **Monitor for:** AI, video technology, content creation, marketing tools queries

#### B. HARO (Now Operated by Featured.com)
- **URL:** https://www.helpareporter.com/
- **Cost:** Free (3 answers/month on free tier)
- **How it works:** Featured.com acquired HARO in April 2025 and relaunched it as a free email digest. Journalists post queries, experts respond. Modern safeguards against spam and AI-generated content.
- **Categories:** Technology, Business, Lifestyle, etc.
- **Sign up:** https://www.helpareporter.com/ (create source account)

#### C. Qwoted
- **URL:** https://www.qwoted.com/
- **Sign up:** https://app.qwoted.com/users/sign_up
- **Cost:** Free (3 queries/month free tier)
- **How it works:** Select "Expert" during signup. Journalists post queries, you respond with credentials + answer. Journalists see your expert card and can message you directly. **Qwoted bans experts who use AI to write pitches** -- write everything yourself.
- **Categories:** Science/Tech, Business, Culture, Money, Products, Health, Politics
- **Why good:** Higher conversion rates than other platforms due to verification filtering. Nearly 100K vetted professionals. Quicker response cycle.

#### D. SourceBottle -- GREAT FOR AUSTRALIA
- **URL:** https://www.sourcebottle.com/
- **Cost:** Free
- **How it works:** Create free Expert Profile to be listed in Expert Directory. Receive daily callout emails from journalists, bloggers, podcasters. Covers US, UK, Ireland, Canada, **Australia and New Zealand**.
- **Categories:** Technology, various industries. Low volume but low competition.
- **Why good:** Australian-origin platform (founded 2009). Loyal journalist base especially outside US. Great for AU-based expert positioning.

#### E. Help a B2B Writer
- **URL:** https://helpab2bwriter.com/
- **Cost:** Free for both sides
- **How it works:** Register as a source, choose area of expertise. Writers submit source requests, platform matches you based on expertise criteria. Only relevant queries delivered.
- **Categories:** B2B technology, marketing, SaaS -- good for AI tools coverage

#### F. Twitter/X #JournoRequest
- **URL:** Search `#JournoRequest` OR `#journorequest` on X/Twitter
- **Cost:** Free
- **How it works:** Journalists post requests with the hashtag. Reply directly or DM. Real-time, no middleman.
- **Categories:** All -- filter for technology, AI, video, marketing

## 6. Data-Driven Content Ideas (Link Magnets)

These original-data posts are designed to be naturally linkable -- the kind of content other bloggers cite as a source.

### 1. "AI Video Generator Speed Test 2026: 15 Tools, Same Prompt, Timed Results"
- **Methodology:** Use identical 100-word prompt across 15 tools. Time generation from submit to complete. Record resolution, quality, file size.
- **Data points:** Generation time (seconds), output resolution, file size, quality score (1-10), cost per generation
- **Why linkable:** No one has done a controlled speed test. Creates a citable benchmark.
- **Estimated effort:** 2-3 days of testing

### 2. "The Real Cost Per Minute of AI Video: 15 Tools Compared (April 2026)"
- **Methodology:** Calculate true cost-per-minute for each tool at lowest and highest tier. Include hidden costs (watermarks, storage, export fees).
- **Data points:** Monthly price, credits/minutes included, cost per minute (low/high), cost per second, annual savings vs monthly, free tier limitations
- **Why linkable:** Pricing changes constantly. A current, accurate comparison with methodology = citation magnet.
- **Estimated effort:** 1-2 days

### 3. "AI Avatar Realism Index: We Scored 10 Platforms on Lip Sync, Expression, and Uncanny Valley"
- **Methodology:** Record same 30-second script across 10 avatar platforms. Have 50 people rate realism on 5 criteria (lip sync, eye contact, gestures, skin texture, uncanny valley score).
- **Data points:** Composite realism score, individual criteria scores, price-to-quality ratio
- **Why linkable:** Original research with human evaluation. Highly shareable visual results.
- **Estimated effort:** 1 week (including survey)

### 4. "State of AI Video 2026: Post-Sora Landscape Report"
- **Methodology:** Track which AI video startups launched, pivoted, shut down, or got acquired in 2025-2026. Map the market with funding data.
- **Data points:** Companies by status (active/dead/acquired/pivoted), total funding raised, market share estimates, technology approach (diffusion vs transformer)
- **Why linkable:** Journalists and analysts cite industry reports. The Sora shutdown narrative is hot right now.
- **Estimated effort:** 3-4 days of research

### 5. "AI Video vs Human Video Producer: Quality, Cost, and Time Comparison"
- **Methodology:** Commission the same 60-second explainer video from: (a) a human freelancer on Fiverr/Upwork, (b) Synthesia, (c) HeyGen, (d) Pictory. Compare results blind.
- **Data points:** Total cost, turnaround time, revision cycles, viewer preference (blind poll), quality criteria scores
- **Why linkable:** Direct human vs AI comparison with real spend data. Extremely shareable.
- **Estimated effort:** 1-2 weeks (waiting for freelancer delivery)
