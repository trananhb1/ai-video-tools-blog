# T21 — Form fix report

**Date**: 2026-04-25
**Owner**: content-writer
**Status**: complete
**Target (T28)**: form_submit / form_start ≥ 30%

## TL;DR
- 53 form_start / 1 form_submit had two stacked causes plus an instrumentation gap.
- Newsletter Kit form (60+ instances) used `target="_blank"`. Clicking Subscribe opened Kit's reCAPTCHA in a *new* tab; the original tab showed nothing. Most users abandoned at the second step.
- `contact.html` shipped with `action="https://formspree.io/f/your-form-id"` — the literal placeholder. Every contact submit went to a 404 silently.
- `contact.html` and `about.html` did not load `assets/js/analytics.js`, so site-wide tracking missed both.
- Fixed all three. New inline newsletter capture below the Quick Answer on both hero pages. Smoke-tested desktop + mobile.

## What was broken

**Kit form UX trap.** Endpoint is fine — `curl -X POST` returns `302 -> /forms/guards/<uuid>` (Kit's reCAPTCHA). Real flow: Subscribe click → new tab opens reCAPTCHA → original tab unchanged → user abandons. 1.9% completion on a 49% mobile audience.

**Contact form placeholder.** `action="https://formspree.io/f/your-form-id"` was never wired. No JS error, no UI, just nothing.

**Analytics gap.** `contact.html` and `about.html` had inline gtag config and skipped `<script src="/assets/js/analytics.js">`, so the outbound tracker (T32) and now the form tracker (T21) didn't run there.

## What changed

**Files touched**
- `assets/js/analytics.js` — added `AVP_FORM_TRACKER_v1`. Site-wide: classifies forms (`newsletter_kit`, `contact`, `contact_formspree`, `contact_mailto`), strips `target="_blank"` from Kit forms at runtime, fires `gtag('event', 'form_start', ...)` on first focus + `form_submit` on submit. Both events carry `form_destination` and `page_path`. `setTimeout(scan, 1500)` rescan catches Kit's `ckjs` late injection.
- `contact.html` — placeholder replaced with `action="mailto:contact@aivideopicks.com" enctype="text/plain"`. Inline gtag swapped for `<script src="/assets/js/analytics.js">`.
- `about.html` — same gtag → analytics.js wiring.
- `posts/best-ai-video-tools-2026.html` — `.avp-inline-capture` block immediately below the Quick Answer. Single email field. Copy: "Get the next AI video tool review first."
- `posts/best-free-ai-video-generators-2026.html` — same block below Quick Answer. Copy: "New free tier? We'll email you first."

**Non-actions.** No edits to the 60+ existing Kit embeds — `target` is stripped at runtime. No third-party signup (mailto works today). No edits to hero URLs or JSON-LD `@id`s. No popup.
